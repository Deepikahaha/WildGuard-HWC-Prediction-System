Safe Observation Best Practices

  • check_circle Maintain a minimum distance of 50 meters from all megafauna.
  • check_circle Stay quiet and avoid sudden movements that might startle animals.
  • check_circle Use binoculars or long-lens cameras for closer details.
  • check_circle Always keep an exit route clear behind you.
warning

DO NOT

  • cancel Never feed wildlife; this leads to dangerous habituation.
  • cancel Do not use flash photography at dusk or dawn.
  • cancel Avoid getting between a mother and her offspring.
  • cancel Do not corner animals against barriers or cliffs.
