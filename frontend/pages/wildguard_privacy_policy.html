Data We Collect

WildGuard is committed to protecting the privacy of our environmental stewards. We only collect essential information during your onboard process to ensure secure data logging and accurate wildlife monitoring.

Name, email, and credentials for secure authentication.

Location Data

GPS coordinates at the time of wildlife sighting reports.

Species details, environmental logs, and field observations.

Photographic evidence uploaded for AI analysis and verification.

Non-identifiable interactions with the dashboard and risk maps.

02

How We Use Your Data

Data collected through WildGuard serves the primary purpose of enhancing wildlife conservation efforts and ensuring park ranger safety through advanced predictive models.

  • hub Prediction Model Enhancement
  • map Real-time Risk Map Calibration
  • model_training YOLO Detection Training
  • emergency_home Automated Risk Alerts
info

We never use your data for advertising or marketing purposes. WildGuard is built solely for stewardship and environmental security.
